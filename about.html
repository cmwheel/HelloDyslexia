Our Story

Empowering Unique Minds

Hello Dyslexia is a California-based nonprofit founded by dyslexic entrepreneurs who get it. We help families navigate screening results with clarity and confidence—because every child deserves to see their different brain as a strength.

Our Mission

From Deficit to Difference

Our Core Mission

Empower every child with dyslexia to see their brain as a superpower—and give families the tools to help them thrive.

Evidence-Based

Grounded in current research and best practices.

Inclusive

For all families, regardless of background.

Empowering

Strengths and potential, not deficits.
