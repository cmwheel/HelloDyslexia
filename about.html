About Us

We believe every child's brain is built for something brilliant.

Hello Dyslexia is a California-based 501(c)(3) (pending) nonprofit helping families see dyslexia for what it really is — a different way of thinking, not a deficit.

Our Story

How it started

Hello Dyslexia is a California-based 501(c)(3) (pending) nonprofit helping families understand and act on dyslexia.

Through evidence-based resources, personalized support plans, and a community that celebrates neurodiversity, we help families navigate screening results with clarity, confidence, and hope.

Every child deserves to thrive using the full potential of how their brilliant brain works.

Our Mission

From deficit to difference.

Empower every child with dyslexia to see their brain as a superpower — and give families the tools to help them thrive.

Evidence-Based

All our resources and recommendations are grounded in current research and best practices.

Inclusive

We serve all families regardless of background, language, or socioeconomic status.

Empowering

We focus on strengths and potential, not deficits and limitations.
