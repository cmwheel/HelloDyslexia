Understand

Dyslexia isn't what you think it is.

01

It's incredibly common.

Up to 1 in 5 children have dyslexia. Your child is in extraordinary company — alongside entrepreneurs, artists, scientists, and leaders.

02

It's not about intelligence.

Dyslexia is a difference in how the brain processes language, not a reflection of how smart your child is. Many gifted thinkers are dyslexic.

03

It comes with superpowers.

Big-picture thinking, creativity, spatial reasoning, and problem-solving — the dyslexic brain is wired for strengths that classrooms often overlook.
