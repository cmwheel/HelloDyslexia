Our Story

Empowering Unique Minds

Hello Dyslexia is a California-based nonprofit founded by dyslexic entrepreneurs who understand firsthand the challenges and incredible strengths that come with different ways of thinking. We started this organization to empower children with dyslexia to embrace their unique minds as their greatest strength, not a limitation.

Through evidence-based resources, personalized support plans, and a community that celebrates neurodiversity, we're helping families navigate dyslexia screening results with clarity, confidence, and hope. Every child deserves to thrive using the full potential of how their brilliant brain works.

Our Mission

Transforming How We See Dyslexia

We're on a mission to change the narrative around dyslexia from deficit to difference.

Our Core Mission

To empower every child with dyslexia to recognize their unique cognitive strengths as superpowers, not weaknesses. We believe that when children understand how their brains work differently, they can unlock their full potential and thrive in a world that often doesn't accommodate neurodiversity.

Evidence-Based

All our resources and recommendations are grounded in current research and best practices.

Inclusive

We serve all families regardless of background, language, or socioeconomic status.

Empowering

We focus on strengths and potential, not deficits and limitations.
